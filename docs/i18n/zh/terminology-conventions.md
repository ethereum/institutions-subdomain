# Terminology Conventions

## Keep in English (untranslated)

These terms are standard in Chinese crypto/finance discourse and should not be translated:

- Layer 2 / L2 / L1
- Rollup
- DeFi
- EVM
- ETH
- DEX / AMM
- TVL / TVS
- ERC-20
- NFT
- DAO
- Gas
- Blob
- ZK / FHE / TEE
- KYC / AML
- OP Stack
- Proof of Human (with gloss on first use: 人格证明)

## Brand Names

Never translate company or product names. Keep exactly as English:

- BlackRock, Coinbase, Visa, Robinhood, Securitize, Aave, Uniswap, etc.
- Base, Linea, Starknet, Scroll, Arbitrum, Optimism, etc.
- VTAP, BENJI, BUIDL, etc.

For well-known companies with established Chinese names, use the Chinese name with English in parentheses on first mention:

- 西门子 (Siemens)
- 法国兴业银行 (Société Générale)
- 新加坡金融管理局 (MAS)
- 摩根大通 (J.P. Morgan)
- 富兰克林邓普顿 (Franklin Templeton)
